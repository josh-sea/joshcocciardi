How to Play Conway's DOOM

🎯 Goal

Navigate to the magenta/yellow checkerboard finish area while avoiding the evolving Conway's Game of Life patterns.

🎮 Movement

Arrow Keys: Move forward/backward and turn left/right

Space: Shoot to destroy walls and living cells

R: Restart the current level

🧬 Conway's Rules

The grid evolves every 3 seconds following Conway's Game of Life rules:

  • Living cells with 2-3 neighbors survive
  • Dead cells with exactly 3 neighbors become alive
  • Living cells with < 2 or > 3 neighbors die

⚠️ Dangers

• Touching living cells deals 10 damage

• You have 1 second of invincibility after taking damage

• Running out of health means game over

💎 Power-ups

• Cyan boxes contain ammo (+10 rounds)

• Each level increases the map size by 3x3

• Completing a level restores 20 health
